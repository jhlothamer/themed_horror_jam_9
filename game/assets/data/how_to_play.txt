The Witch and her minions must complete the witch’s brew before they are overrun by what creeps in the fog beyond!

Controls:

Left Click the Witch or Minion to Take Control

Right click a location to move to it

Right Click a Work Station to Interact

Work Stations:

Cauldron: Interact with the Cauldron to refine the Witches Brew

Wood Pile: Interact as a minion to gain the ability to repair a broken part of your cabin

Doors/Windows: Interact after interacting with the Wood Pile to repair if they’re broken

Crystal Ball: Monitor the exterior of a cabin and use [Q] [E] or [<] [>] to scroll through your exterior views. Use [ Space Bar ] to place wards to fend off incoming enemies

Mana Pool: Interact with the mana pool to gain mana and the ability to shoot undead who have invaded your cabin as well as place wards outside your cabin. Once you have gained mana, right click an enemy to shoot it with your imbued magic powers

Spellbook: Interact with the spellbook to unlock its mysteries…
